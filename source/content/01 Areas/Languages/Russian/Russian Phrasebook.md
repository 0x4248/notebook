# Russian Phrasebook

***
Here is a list of common words and phrases in Russian. 

## Greetings

| Russian    | Pronunciation | English   |
| ---------- | ------------- | --------- |
| Привет     | Privet        | Hello     |
| Пока       | Poka          | Bye       |
| Спасибо    | Spasibo       | Thanks    |
| Пожалуйста | Pozhaluysta   | Please    |
| Извините   | Izvinite      | Excuse me |
