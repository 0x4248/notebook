# Two's Complement
***
Two's compliment allows us to convert numbers from positive to negative and vice versa.
### Steps
1. Flip all the bits.
2. Add 1.

### Example
```
00000011 = 3

FLIP 11111100
ADD  00000001
------------
     11111101 = -3
    
```
