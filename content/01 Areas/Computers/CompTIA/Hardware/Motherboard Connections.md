# Motherboard Connections

***
- **PCI**
    - Older and has slower transfer rates
- **PCIe**
    - High speed slot replacing PCIe
- **M.2**:
    - High speed for SSD's and WiFi and Bluetooth cards
- 
## Power connectors
- **24 Pin ATX**: Power to motherboard
- **CPU Pin Power**: CPU
- **PCIe 6/8 Pin**: GPU Power
