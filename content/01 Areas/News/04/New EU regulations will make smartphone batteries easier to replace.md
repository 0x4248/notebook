<p class="dropcap tech">A new regulation published in 2023 will mean that smartphone batteries will need to be designed with "repairability" and "replaceability". Is it set on February 17, 2027 for the new regulations to come into effect. This means major phones like the iPhone could see a complete redesign in 2027. Some phones already comply with the regulation enough, but with apples parts pairing it will violate what the EU has set out.</p>
<p style="font-size:x-large;text-align:center;margin:0;font-family:sanserif;color:lightblue;"><i>“Any natural or legal person that places on the market products incorporating portable batteries shall ensure that those batteries are readily removable and replaceable by the end-user”</i></p>
